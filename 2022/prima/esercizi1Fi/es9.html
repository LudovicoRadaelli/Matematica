Esercizio 1

\[ \dfrac{1}{9x^2 -4} - \dfrac{2 -x}{27x^3 + 8} \]

Soluzione

\(\dfrac{12x^2 -14x +8}{\left(3x -2\right)\cdot\left(3x +2\right)\cdot\left(9x^2 -6x +4\right)}\)



Esercizio 2

\[ \dfrac{-x^3 +5x^2 +4x -20}{-x^2 +3x +10} \cdot \dfrac{-4}{x^2 -2x} + \dfrac{7x -x^2}{49 -x^2} \]


Soluzione

\(\dfrac{x^2 -4x -28}{x\cdot\left(x +7\right)}\)



Esercizio 3

\[ \dfrac{6x +6}{12x^2 +24x +12} : \dfrac{7x -1}{x^3 +1} - \dfrac{2-x}{6} \cdot \dfrac{12x}{4 -x^2} \]


Soluzione

\(\dfrac{x^3 -27x^2 +3x +2}{2\left(x +2\right)\left(7x -1\right)}\)
