Esercizio 1

Calcolare i seguenti limiti:


  1. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow 1}{lim}\,\,3x^2 + x + 1 \]

  2. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow 2}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{1 + x}{4 + x} \]

  3. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -4}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{1 + x}{4 + x} \]

  4. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -3}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{1 + 3x}{\left(x + 3\right)^2} \]

  5. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow 2}{lim}\,\,f(x) \qquad \text{con}\,\,\, f(x) = \begin{cases} -5x + 1 \quad\text{se}\,\,x \lt 2 \\\\ \dfrac{5x}{x - 2} \quad\text{se}\,\,x \gt 2 \end{cases} \]

  6. \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -5}{lim}\,\,f(x) \qquad \text{con}\,\,\, f(x) = \begin{cases} 2x \quad\text{se}\,\,x \lt -5 \\\\ -2x - 20 \quad\text{se}\,\,x \gt -5 \end{cases} \]
