Esercizio 1

Calcolare i seguenti limiti

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{4x^5 + 3x^4 + x^2 -11}{2x^5 - 5x^3 + x + 7} \]

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{4x^5 + 3x^4 + x^2 -11}{2x^5 - 5x^3 + x + 7} \]


Soluzione:

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = 2 \]


  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = 2 \]




Esercizio 2

Calcolare i seguenti limiti

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{-4x^2 + 3x -1}{x -1} \]

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{-4x^2 + 3x -1}{x -1} \]


Soluzione:

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = -\infty \]


  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = +\infty \]




Esercizio 3

Calcolare i seguenti limiti

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{x^9 -10}{x^10 +10} \]

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,\dfrac{x^9 -10}{x^10 +10} \]


Soluzione:

  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow +\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = 0 \]


  • \[ \underset{x \longrightarrow -\infty}{lim}\,\,f(x) = 0 \]
