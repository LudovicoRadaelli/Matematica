Esercizio 1

Stabilire il valore delle seguenti funzioni trigonometriche in corrispondenza degli angoli indicati

    \[ cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right) \qquad sin\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{11}{3}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\pi\right) \qquad sin\left(\dfrac{4}{3}\pi\right) \]

    \[ cos\left(\dfrac{9}{4}\pi\right) \qquad sin\left(\dfrac{11}{6}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{10}{4}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right) \qquad sin\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\pi\right) \]

    \[cos\left(\dfrac{5}{4}\pi\right) \qquad tan\left(\dfrac{11}{3}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\pi\right) \qquad sin\left(0\right) \qquad tan\left(\pi\right)\]

    \[ cos\left(\dfrac{9}{4}\pi\right) \qquad sin\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{3}{2}\pi\right) \qquad tan\left(\pi\right) \qquad cos\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\pi\right) \]
