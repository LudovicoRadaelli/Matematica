Formule goniometriche

Formule di duplicazione

\( sin\left(2\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} \right)= 2\,\, sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \,\, cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \)


\( \begin{align*} cos\left(2\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) = \,\,& cos^2\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) - sin^2\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) = \\\\ = \,\,& 2\,cos^2\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) - 1 = \\\\ = \,\, & 1 - 2\,sin^2\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) = \end{align*} \)


\( tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) = \dfrac{tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) }{1 - tan^2\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right)} \)



Formule di bisezione

Nelle formule che seguono il segno viene scelto in base all'ampiezza dell'angolo \(\dfrac{\color{red}{\alpha}}{2}\)


\( sin\left(\dfrac{\color{red}{\alpha}}{2}\right) = \pm \sqrt{\dfrac{1 - cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right)}{2}} \)


\( cos\left(\dfrac{\color{red}{\alpha}}{2}\right) = \pm \sqrt{\dfrac{1 + cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right)}{2}} \)


\( tan\left(\dfrac{\color{red}{\alpha}}{2}\right) = \pm \sqrt{\dfrac{1 - cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right)}{1 + cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right)}} \)
