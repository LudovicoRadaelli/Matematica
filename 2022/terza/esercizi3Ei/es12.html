Formule goniometriche

Formule di addizione

\[ sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} + \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) + sin\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \]

\[ cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} + \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) - sin\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \]

\[ tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} + \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = \dfrac{tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) + tan\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right)}{1 - tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot tan\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right)} \]


Formule di sottrazione

\[ sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} - \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) - sin\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \]

\[ cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} - \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = cos\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot cos\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) + sin\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot sin\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \]

\[ tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{} - \color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right) = \dfrac{tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) - tan\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right)}{1 + tan\left(\color{red}{\alpha}\color{black}{}\right) \cdot tan\left(\color{blue}{\beta}\color{black}{}\right)} \]
